Finding the right used auto part starts with telling AUAPW exactly what you drive. Whether you work through a guided year-make-model selector or paste in your VIN, AUAPW's search engine filters its entire inventory of verified salvage-yard listings down to only the parts confirmed to fit your vehicle — saving you time and protecting you from costly returns.

## Find Your Part

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Search by Vehicle">
    Use the guided vehicle selector when you know your car's year, make, and model but don't have your VIN handy. Each step narrows the results automatically.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Select Your Year">
        On the AUAPW homepage or the **Search Parts** page, open the **Year** dropdown and choose your vehicle's model year.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select Your Make">
        Choose your vehicle's manufacturer from the **Make** dropdown. The list updates to show only makes available for the year you selected.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select Your Model">
        Pick your specific model from the **Model** dropdown. If your make produces multiple body styles for the same nameplate, each appears as a separate option.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select Your Trim">
        Choose your trim level from the **Trim** dropdown. Trim selection is critical — suspension, brake, and electrical components often differ between trim levels on the same model.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enter a Part Name or Category">
        Type the part name (e.g., *alternator*, *front bumper cover*) into the search bar, or browse by category using the category tiles below the search bar. Press **Search** to view matching listings.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Search by VIN">
    Your VIN encodes every factory specification for your vehicle, making it the most precise way to search. Use this method for parts where trim or option-package differences matter most.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Locate Your 17-Digit VIN">
        Find your VIN on the driver-side door jamb, the lower-left corner of your windshield dashboard, your vehicle registration, or your insurance card.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enter Your VIN in the Search Field">
        Click **Search by VIN** on the AUAPW search page, paste or type your full 17-character VIN into the field, and press **Decode VIN**. AUAPW will automatically populate your year, make, model, and trim.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Browse Matching Parts">
        With your vehicle decoded, enter a part name or select a category. Every listing returned has been cross-referenced against your exact VIN specification — results marked with a green **Fits Your Vehicle** badge are confirmed compatible.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Tip>
      Using your VIN is the most reliable search method. It captures factory-installed options and regional variants that a standard year-make-model search can miss — especially useful for engines, transmissions, and electronic control modules.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Note>
  Your VIN is printed on a sticker on the **driver-side door jamb** (visible when the door is open) and etched into a metal plate on the **top of the dashboard**, visible through the windshield from outside the vehicle. It is always exactly 17 characters long and contains no letters I, O, or Q.
</Note>

## Filtering Results

After your initial search, use the filter panel on the left side of the results page to narrow listings by the criteria that matter most to you:

- **Condition** — Choose from *Used*, *Reconditioned*, or *Remanufactured* to match your quality and budget needs.
- **Price Range** — Set a minimum and maximum price to surface listings within your budget.
- **Seller Rating** — Filter by seller star rating (e.g., 4 stars and above) to prioritize highly rated salvage yards.
- **Distance / Location** — Limit results by proximity to your ZIP code to reduce shipping costs or arrange local pickup.
- **Mileage** — Set a maximum mileage threshold to ensure the part comes from a low-mileage donor vehicle.
