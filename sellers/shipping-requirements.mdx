How you package and ship a part is just as important as the accuracy of your listing. A correctly described part that arrives damaged due to poor packaging, or that ships three days late, results in a negative buyer experience — and that experience is reflected directly in your seller rating. Meeting AUAPW's shipping requirements protects your rating, reduces returns, and keeps buyers coming back to your store.

## Handling Time Requirements

AUAPW requires all sellers to ship confirmed orders within **2 business days** of order placement. "Ship" means a carrier scan has been recorded on the tracking number — not just that you've generated a label.

**Handling time expectations:**

| Handling Time | Rating Impact |
|---|---|
| Same business day | Positive impact — boosts handling score |
| Next business day | Meets standard — neutral impact |
| 2 business days | Meets minimum — neutral impact |
| 3+ business days | Negative impact — flags your account |
| No shipment after 5 days | Order auto-cancelled — significant rating penalty |

<Info>
Business days are Monday through Friday, excluding federal holidays. If you receive an order on Friday afternoon, your 2-business-day window begins the following Monday. Set up order notification alerts in **Account Settings → Notifications** so you never miss an incoming order.
</Info>

## Approved Carriers

AUAPW supports the following carriers for shipping. All shipments must use a carrier that provides real-time tracking and electronic proof of delivery.

**Standard Parcel Shipping (under 75 lbs):**
- **UPS** (Ground, 2-Day Air, Overnight)
- **FedEx** (Ground, 2Day, Priority Overnight)
- **USPS** (Priority Mail, Priority Mail Express, Parcel Select)

**Freight Shipping (over 75 lbs or oversized):**
- Any reputable **LTL (Less-Than-Truckload) freight carrier** for heavy items such as engines, transmissions, differentials, and axle assemblies
- Common options include FedEx Freight, UPS Freight, XPO Logistics, and Estes Express
- Freight shipments require a Bill of Lading (BOL); see the Freight Shipments section below

<Warning>
Do not use untracked or signature-waived shipping methods for orders over $100. AUAPW's seller protection policy requires a valid tracking number with carrier-confirmed delivery for all dispute resolution. Orders shipped without tracking are ineligible for seller protection in the event of a buyer dispute.
</Warning>

## Generating Shipping Labels

You can generate discounted shipping labels directly from your Seller Dashboard without leaving the platform. Labels generated through AUAPW are pre-addressed with the buyer's shipping information and include tracking that auto-updates the order status.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Your Seller Dashboard">
    Log in at [auapw.org/dashboard](https://auapw.org/dashboard) and navigate to **Orders → Pending Shipment**. All orders awaiting a label are listed here.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Order">
    Click the order number to open the order detail page. Confirm the buyer's shipping address and the part being shipped before generating the label.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Generate Label'">
    Click the **Generate Label** button. AUAPW will pull the package dimensions and weight from your listing data. You can edit these values here if the actual packaged weight differs from what you listed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Carrier and Service Level">
    Choose your preferred carrier (UPS, FedEx, or USPS) and the service level. AUAPW displays the cost to you for each option. Buyers paid the shipping rate shown at checkout, which was calculated from your listing dimensions — any difference is absorbed or credited based on your seller tier agreement.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Print and Attach the Label">
    Click **Purchase and Print Label**. The label opens as a PDF. Print it on a standard 4×6 label or on letter-size paper (fold and tape). Attach the label securely to the outside of the package, ensuring the barcode is fully visible and not covered by tape.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Professional and Enterprise sellers have access to AUAPW's **discounted carrier rates** — negotiated rates with UPS, FedEx, and USPS that are typically 15–40% below retail rates. These savings are passed directly to you when you generate labels through the dashboard. Standard sellers can upgrade to Professional at any time from **Account Settings → Seller Tier** to unlock this benefit.
</Tip>

## Packaging Standards

Proper packaging prevents damage in transit and protects you from return claims. Follow these standards for every shipment:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Drain All Fluids" icon="droplet" href="/sellers/shipping-requirements">
    All parts that contain or could contain fluids — engines, transmissions, fuel pumps, radiators, coolant reservoirs — must be fully drained and cleaned before shipping. Leaking packages are refused by carriers and can result in hazmat violations.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Wrap Metal Parts" icon="box" href="/sellers/shipping-requirements">
    Wrap metal parts in at least two layers of bubble wrap or closed-cell foam padding. Pay special attention to connectors, sensors, threaded ports, and any fragile protrusions that could break off or puncture the box.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Use Appropriate Boxes" icon="boxes-stacked" href="/sellers/shipping-requirements">
    Use **double-walled (double-flute) corrugated boxes** for parts weighing more than 20 lbs. Single-wall boxes are acceptable for lighter items. Reused boxes are permitted as long as they are structurally sound and all old labels and barcodes are removed or covered.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Include a Packing Slip" icon="file-lines" href="/sellers/shipping-requirements">
    Print and place a packing slip inside every box. The packing slip should include the order number, part name, OEM number, and your store name. This helps buyers reconcile deliveries and speeds up any support inquiries.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

**Additional packaging requirements:**
- Fill empty box space with packing peanuts, crumpled kraft paper, or air pillows to prevent shifting in transit
- For glass parts (headlights, mirrors, windshields), use foam-in-place packaging or purpose-built foam inserts — bubble wrap alone is insufficient
- Palletize freight items: stack on a standard 48×40-inch pallet, shrink-wrap with at least 4 layers of stretch film, and secure with strapping if the load exceeds 500 lbs

## Freight Shipments

Use LTL freight for any single item or order that exceeds **75 lbs** or has dimensions that exceed standard parcel carrier limits (typically 165 inches in combined length + girth).

**Common freight items include:**
- Complete engines and engine assemblies
- Automatic and manual transmissions
- Rear axle assemblies and differentials
- Full doors with glass and hardware
- Complete dashboards and instrument panels

**To ship freight:**

1. Weigh and measure the palletized, packaged item
2. Go to **Orders → Pending Shipment** and click **Ship via Freight**
3. Enter the freight dimensions and weight to receive a rate quote
4. Schedule a carrier pickup or arrange drop-off at the freight terminal
5. Upload the Bill of Lading (BOL) number to the order so tracking updates automatically

**Liftgate requirements:** Always note in the order whether the delivery address requires a liftgate (most residential addresses and many small businesses do). Failing to request a liftgate when needed can result in a failed delivery attempt, extra carrier fees, and a frustrated buyer. When in doubt, select liftgate.

<Warning>
Parts that arrive damaged due to inadequate packaging — including fluid leaks, broken connectors, or crushed housings caused by under-packed boxes — are the **seller's responsibility**. AUAPW will approve the return, and the cost of the return shipment will be deducted from your payout. Repeated packaging-related returns will trigger a mandatory packaging review and may result in account suspension.
</Warning>
