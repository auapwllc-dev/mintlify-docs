All Used Auto Parts Warehouse (AUAPW) connects licensed auto recyclers, salvage yards, and parts dealers with buyers across the country — giving your inventory a nationwide audience without the overhead of building your own e-commerce platform. Once your account is verified, you can publish listings, receive orders, and collect payouts entirely through your Seller Dashboard.

## Who Can Sell

AUAPW is a business-to-consumer and business-to-business marketplace designed for professional parts sellers. Eligible seller types include:

- **Licensed salvage yards** operating under a state-issued salvage or dismantler license
- **Auto recyclers** registered with a recognized recycling program or association
- **Used auto parts dealers** with a valid business license and physical parts inventory

<Warning>
AUAPW does not permit individuals to list parts removed from personally owned vehicles. Every seller must hold an active business license and a federal Employer Identification Number (EIN). Applications without valid credentials will be declined during the verification review.
</Warning>

## What You'll Need

Before you begin your application, gather the following documents and information:

| Requirement | Details |
|---|---|
| **Business License** | Current, state-issued license authorizing you to sell auto parts |
| **Tax ID (EIN)** | Federal Employer Identification Number for your business entity |
| **Business Address** | Physical address of your salvage yard or warehouse |
| **Bank Account** | U.S. checking account to receive weekly ACH payouts |

Having these on hand before you start the application will speed up the review process and help you clear verification in the standard 24–48 hour window.

## Application Steps

Follow the steps below to create your seller account and go live on AUAPW.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Seller Application Page">
    Go to [auapw.org/sell](https://auapw.org/sell) and click **Apply to Sell**. You will be prompted to create an account with a business email address and a secure password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Business Information">
    Provide your legal business name, DBA name (if applicable), business phone number, physical address, and website URL. Make sure the address matches the one on your business license.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload Required Documents">
    Upload a clear, legible scan or photo of your **business license** and your **EIN confirmation letter** (IRS Form CP 575 or equivalent). Files must be in PDF, JPG, or PNG format and under 10 MB each.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the Seller Agreement">
    Read and electronically sign the AUAPW Seller Agreement, which covers listing policies, fee terms, fulfillment obligations, and the returns process. You must accept the agreement to proceed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for Verification">
    AUAPW's seller onboarding team will review your application and documents. Verification typically completes within **24–48 business hours**. You will receive an email notification when your account is approved or if additional information is needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Up Your Storefront Profile">
    Once approved, log in to your Seller Dashboard and complete your storefront profile. Add your business logo, a brief description of your inventory specialty, your operating hours, and your return policy statement. A complete profile builds buyer trust.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Listing Parts">
    You're ready to add inventory. Use the **Add New Part** tool in your dashboard to create individual listings, or use the bulk CSV upload if you're a Professional or Enterprise seller with a large catalog.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
After you submit your application, check the inbox of your business email for a confirmation message from **no-reply@auapw.org**. If you don't see it within 15 minutes, check your spam folder. Document review is conducted during business hours (Monday–Friday, 8 AM–6 PM CT), so applications submitted on weekends will begin processing the following Monday.
</Note>

## Seller Tiers

AUAPW offers three seller tiers to accommodate businesses of different sizes. You start on the Standard tier automatically and can upgrade at any time from your account settings.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Standard" icon="store" href="/sellers/pricing-guidelines">
    **Up to 500 active listings**

    Free for first 100 listings/month, then $0.10 per listing. Email support with standard response times. Ideal for small salvage yards and single-location dealers.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Professional" icon="star" href="/sellers/pricing-guidelines">
    **Unlimited listings**

    Priority customer support, access to promotional tools (discounts and sales), and bulk CSV inventory upload. Best for multi-location dealers and high-volume recyclers.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Enterprise" icon="building" href="/sellers/pricing-guidelines">
    **Unlimited listings + API access**

    Full REST API for direct inventory sync, dedicated account manager, bulk upload, and discounted carrier shipping rates. Designed for large-scale operations with existing inventory management systems.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
If you manage inventory in a third-party system (e.g., Hollander, Car-Part Pro, or a custom IMS), the Enterprise tier's API integration can automate listing creation and status updates, eliminating manual data entry entirely.
</Tip>
