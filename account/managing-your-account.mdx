Your AUAPW account is the control center for everything you do on the platform — from searching parts and tracking orders to managing the vehicles you own. Keeping your profile up to date ensures you get accurate fitment results, timely shipping notifications, and a smooth checkout every time you shop.

## Profile Settings

Your profile stores the basic contact details sellers and our support team use to reach you. To update your information, log in and navigate to **Account → Profile Settings**.

From this page you can update:

- **Full name** — used on shipping labels and invoices
- **Email address** — used for login and order notifications (see note below)
- **Phone number** — used for SMS notifications and seller contact

<Note>
  Changing your email address triggers a verification email to the new address. Your account remains linked to your old email until you click the confirmation link in that message.
</Note>

## Password & Security

Keeping your account secure protects your order history, saved addresses, and payment details. You can update your credentials at any time from **Account → Security**.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Change Your Password">
    Navigate to **Account → Security → Change Password**. Enter your current password, then type and confirm your new password. Passwords must be at least 8 characters and include one number or special character.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)">
    Under **Security**, toggle on **Two-Factor Authentication**. Choose between receiving a one-time code by SMS or using an authenticator app such as Google Authenticator or Authy. Scan the QR code or enter the setup key, then confirm with your first generated code.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Active Sessions">
    Scroll to **Active Sessions** to see all devices currently logged in to your account. Select **Sign Out** next to any session you don't recognize.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Saved Addresses

Adding multiple shipping addresses saves time at checkout — especially if you order parts for a home garage and a separate shop. Go to **Account → Addresses** to manage your saved locations.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add a New Address">
    Click **Add Address**, fill in the full street address, city, state, and ZIP code, then click **Save**. You can store as many addresses as you need.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set a Default Address">
    Click the **⋯** menu next to any saved address and select **Set as Default**. Your default address pre-fills at checkout but can still be changed before you place an order.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit or Delete an Address">
    Select **Edit** to update any field, or **Delete** to remove the address entirely. Deleting an address does not affect any existing orders associated with it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  P.O. Boxes are not accepted for large freight items such as engines, transmissions, or body panels. Make sure your default address is a physical street address if you regularly order heavy parts.
</Warning>

## Saved Vehicles

Adding your vehicles to your account lets AUAPW automatically filter search results to parts that fit your specific year, make, and model — cutting out the guesswork. You can save up to **10 vehicles** in your garage.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add a Vehicle">
    Go to **Account → My Garage → Add Vehicle**. Select the year, make, model, and trim from the dropdown menus, then click **Save Vehicle**. You can also add a personal nickname (e.g., "Daily Driver" or "Weekend Project") to keep things organized.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Switch Between Vehicles">
    On any search results page, use the **Garage** selector in the top navigation to instantly switch the active vehicle filter. Results update immediately to show compatible parts for the selected vehicle.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Remove a Vehicle">
    In **My Garage**, click **Remove** next to any vehicle you no longer own. This does not affect past orders or searches tied to that vehicle.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Save every vehicle you own or regularly work on — even project cars you only buy parts for occasionally. Switching between profiles takes one click, and you'll always get compatibility-filtered results without re-entering year/make/model every time.
</Tip>

## Notification Preferences

AUAPW sends updates about your orders, shipments, and account activity. You decide how and when you hear from us. Go to **Account → Notifications** to customize your preferences.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Email Notifications" icon="envelope" href="/account/managing-your-account">
    Toggle email alerts for order confirmations, shipping updates, delivery confirmations, return status, and promotional offers. Transactional emails (order confirmations, receipts) cannot be disabled.
  </Card>
  <Card title="SMS Notifications" icon="mobile" href="/account/managing-your-account">
    Opt in to text message alerts for shipping and delivery updates. Standard messaging rates from your carrier may apply. You can opt out at any time by texting STOP in reply to any AUAPW message.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Info>
  Promotional emails include exclusive deals from verified salvage yards. You can unsubscribe from marketing emails without affecting order and shipping notifications.
</Info>

## Closing Your Account

If you'd like to permanently delete your AUAPW account, you can submit a deletion request from your account settings. Before you do, review what this means for your data.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Submit a Deletion Request">
    Navigate to **Account → Settings → Close Account**. Read the confirmation notice, then click **Request Account Deletion**. You'll receive a confirmation email within 24 hours.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the Request">
    Click the confirmation link in the email within 48 hours. If you don't confirm, the deletion request will expire and your account will remain active.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Understand What Happens to Your Data">
    After deletion, your personal profile data is removed from our systems within 30 days. However, AUAPW retains anonymized order records as required for financial and legal compliance. Sellers retain copies of invoices for their own records.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Account deletion is permanent. Once confirmed, you cannot recover your saved vehicles, addresses, order history, or seller reviews. Download any invoices you may need before proceeding.
</Warning>
