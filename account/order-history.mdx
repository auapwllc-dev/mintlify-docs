Every order you place while logged in to your AUAPW account is saved to your order history, giving you a permanent record of parts purchased, sellers you've worked with, and shipments received. Whether you need to track a package, grab a receipt for an insurance claim, or reorder a part that wore out again, your order history puts everything within a few clicks.

## Accessing Order History

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log In to Your Account">
    Visit [auapw.org](https://auapw.org) and click **Sign In** in the top-right corner. Enter your email and password, then complete any two-factor authentication prompt if you have 2FA enabled.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Your Profile Menu">
    Click the **profile icon** (a silhouette or your avatar) in the top navigation bar to open the account dropdown menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select 'My Orders'">
    Click **My Orders** from the dropdown. You'll land on your order history page, which lists all orders in reverse chronological order — most recent first.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Orders placed as a guest do not appear in order history. If you checked out without an account, refer to the confirmation email you received at the time of purchase for your order number, tracking information, and receipt.
</Note>

## What You Can Do From Order History

Each order in your history has a detail page that gives you direct actions without needing to contact support. Click any order number to open its detail view.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="View Order Status & Tracking" icon="truck" href="/ordering/order-tracking">
    See real-time order status — processing, shipped, in transit, or delivered. Click the tracking number to open the carrier's tracking page in a new tab.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Download Invoice / Receipt" icon="file-invoice" href="/account/order-history">
    Generate and download a PDF invoice for any completed order. Invoices include the part details, seller information, price paid, taxes, and shipping cost.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Contact the Seller" icon="message" href="/support/contact-us">
    Send a direct message to the selling salvage yard from the order detail page. Use this for questions about the part's condition, fitment, or shipping timeline before filing a formal dispute.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Initiate a Return Request" icon="rotate-left" href="/shipping/returns-policy">
    Start the return process directly from the order detail page. Select a reason, upload any required photos, and submit. The seller has 2 business days to respond with return instructions.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Leave a Seller Review" icon="star" href="/account/order-history">
    After your order is marked delivered, a **Leave a Review** prompt appears. Rate the seller on accuracy, communication, and shipping speed to help other buyers make confident decisions.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Reorder the Same Part" icon="cart-plus" href="/ordering/placing-an-order">
    Click **Reorder** to add the exact same part (year, make, model, part number) back to your cart. Availability depends on current seller inventory — if the original listing is sold, AUAPW will surface similar listings automatically.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Filtering and Searching Orders

If you've placed many orders over time, the search and filter tools at the top of the My Orders page help you find what you need quickly.

- **Search by keyword** — enter a part name, part number, seller name, or order number in the search bar
- **Filter by status** — narrow results to Processing, Shipped, Delivered, Returned, or Cancelled
- **Filter by date range** — set a custom start and end date, or choose a preset window (Last 30 Days, Last 6 Months, Last Year)
- **Filter by seller** — if you regularly buy from the same salvage yard, select their name to view only orders from that seller

Filters can be combined. For example, you can filter for all **Delivered** orders from a specific seller in the **Last Year** to quickly audit your purchase history.

## Order Receipts

AUAPW generates a PDF receipt for every completed order that you can download at any time — there's no expiration on receipt availability.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Order Detail Page">
    From **My Orders**, click the order number for the purchase you need a receipt for.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Download Invoice'">
    Near the top of the order detail page, click the **Download Invoice** button. Your browser will download a PDF file named with your order number (e.g., `AUAPW-20847-invoice.pdf`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use the Receipt as Needed">
    The PDF is formatted for expense reporting, insurance documentation, and reimbursement submissions. It includes your shipping address, the seller's business name, itemized costs, and applicable taxes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Writing off vehicle maintenance costs? Download receipts for all parts purchases at tax time using the **date range filter** to pull the full year's orders at once, then download invoices for each one. Consider leaving seller reviews while you're there — it only takes a moment and directly helps other buyers in the AUAPW community find trustworthy sellers.
</Tip>
