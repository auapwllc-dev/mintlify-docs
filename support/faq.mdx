Finding and buying used auto parts online raises a lot of questions — about fitment, payment, shipping, and what happens if something goes wrong. The answers below cover the questions AUAPW buyers ask most often. If you don't find what you're looking for here, our support team is ready to help via live chat or email.

## Searching & Compatibility

<Accordion title="How do I know if a part will fit my vehicle?">
  The most reliable way to confirm fitment is to add your vehicle to your **My Garage** profile (year, make, model, and trim). AUAPW automatically filters search results to parts that are compatible with your saved vehicle based on manufacturer interchange data.

  You can also enter your vehicle details in the search bar before running a search. If you're still unsure, check the part listing for a **Fits These Vehicles** compatibility chart, or message the seller directly with your year, make, model, trim, and engine size before purchasing.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What is an interchange number?">
  An interchange number is a standardized code that identifies parts across different vehicle makes, models, and years that are physically interchangeable. For example, a door mirror from a 2015 Ford F-150 may share an interchange number with mirrors from several other F-150 model years, meaning they're functionally identical and can be swapped.

  Searching by interchange number is one of the most accurate ways to find a compatible part. You can find interchange numbers in your vehicle's repair manual, on parts lookup sites, or by asking the seller.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I search by part number?">
  Yes. Enter the OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) part number directly into the search bar. AUAPW will return listings that match or cross-reference that number. If no exact match appears, try searching by the part name and your vehicle year/make/model instead, or contact a seller who stocks that type of part.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What does 'Remanufactured' mean?">
  A remanufactured part (sometimes called "reman") has been professionally rebuilt to meet original factory specifications. This typically involves disassembling the part, replacing worn or failed components, testing, and reassembling it. Remanufactured parts are a middle option between new OEM parts and used-as-removed parts — they're generally more reliable than standard used parts and less expensive than brand-new ones.

  On AUAPW listings, part condition is always listed clearly: **Used**, **Remanufactured**, or **New Aftermarket**.
</Accordion>

## Ordering & Payment

<Accordion title="Do I need an account to buy?">
  No — you can check out as a guest without creating an account. However, creating a free account gives you access to order history, saved vehicles, saved addresses, seller messaging, and the ability to initiate returns directly from your dashboard. Guest orders are confirmed by email only and cannot be accessed through My Orders later.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What payment methods are accepted?">
  AUAPW accepts all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and Apple Pay. Payment is processed securely at checkout using industry-standard encryption. AUAPW does not store your full card number after a transaction is complete.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I cancel or change my order?">
  You can request a cancellation or modification as long as the seller has not yet shipped the part. Go to **My Orders**, open the order, and click **Request Cancellation**. If the order has already shipped, you'll need to wait for delivery and then initiate a return.

  Changes to shipping address, part quantity, or part selection after an order is placed must be requested through the seller message tool — modifications are at the seller's discretion.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is my payment information secure?">
  Yes. All payment data is encrypted in transit using TLS and processed through PCI-DSS-compliant payment providers. AUAPW never stores your full credit card number, CVV, or bank account details on its servers. You can shop with confidence that your financial information is protected.
</Accordion>

## Shipping & Delivery

<Accordion title="How long does shipping take?">
  Shipping times vary by seller location, part size, and the shipping method selected at checkout. Most standard parts (e.g., alternators, sensors, interior components) ship within **1–3 business days** of order confirmation and arrive within **3–7 business days** after that.

  Each listing displays the seller's estimated handling time and available shipping speeds. You can see a delivery estimate on the product page before you add the item to your cart.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How are large parts like engines shipped?">
  Heavy or oversized parts — including engines, transmissions, rear axle assemblies, and large body panels — are typically shipped via **freight carrier** (LTL shipping) on a pallet. Freight shipments require a delivery address where a forklift or loading dock is available, or you can opt for **liftgate delivery** (usually a small additional fee) if you're receiving at a residence or standard driveway.

  Freight delivery windows are scheduled by the carrier and typically require someone to be present to accept the shipment. The seller will provide the freight tracking number and carrier contact information once the item ships.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What happens if my part arrives damaged?">
  If your part arrives damaged, take clear photos of both the packaging and the part itself before moving or installing it. Then:

  1. Go to **My Orders** and open the affected order.
  2. Click **Report a Problem → Item Arrived Damaged**.
  3. Upload your photos and describe the damage.
  4. Submit the report — the seller has 2 business days to respond with a resolution (replacement, refund, or partial credit).

  If the seller does not respond or the resolution is unsatisfactory, AUAPW support will step in to mediate.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do you ship internationally?">
  Currently, AUAPW sellers ship within the **United States and Canada** only. International shipping outside of these two countries is not supported at this time. If you have a specific international inquiry, contact our support team at support@auapw.org.
</Accordion>

## Returns & Refunds

<Accordion title="What is the return policy?">
  Most parts sold on AUAPW are eligible for return within **30 days of delivery**, provided the part has not been installed, modified, or damaged after receipt. Each seller may set additional conditions — check the **Return Policy** section on any product listing before purchasing.

  Parts that arrive damaged, are incorrect, or don't match the listing description are always eligible for a return or refund regardless of the seller's individual policy.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I start a return?">
  Log in and go to **My Orders**. Open the order you want to return, click **Request Return**, select a return reason from the dropdown, and add any relevant photos or notes. Submit the request — the seller will review it and provide a return shipping label or instructions within 2 business days.

  Do not ship the part back before receiving return authorization from the seller, as unauthorized returns may not be accepted.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="When will I receive my refund?">
  Once the seller confirms receipt of the returned part and approves the refund, AUAPW processes the refund to your original payment method within **3–5 business days**. Depending on your bank or card issuer, the funds may take an additional 2–5 business days to appear in your account.

  You'll receive an email confirmation when the refund is initiated. You can also check the status at any time from the order detail page in **My Orders**.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What parts are non-returnable?">
  The following part types are generally non-returnable once received:

  - **Electrical components** (ECUs, control modules, sensors) that show signs of installation
  - **Airbags and safety restraint components**
  - **Hazardous materials** (batteries, fluids)
  - **Special-order parts** that were sourced specifically for your request
  - Any part that has been **installed, modified, or disassembled**

  Always check the individual listing's return policy before purchasing. If you're unsure whether a part is returnable, contact the seller with your questions before placing the order.
</Accordion>

## Account

<Accordion title="How do I reset my password?">
  On the sign-in page, click **Forgot Password?** below the password field. Enter the email address associated with your account and click **Send Reset Link**. Check your inbox for an email from AUAPW — click the reset link inside and follow the prompts to set a new password. The link expires after 60 minutes.

  If you don't see the email, check your spam or junk folder. If it still hasn't arrived after a few minutes, contact support@auapw.org.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I save multiple vehicle profiles?">
  Yes — you can save up to **10 vehicles** in your AUAPW garage. Go to **Account → My Garage → Add Vehicle** and enter the year, make, model, and trim for each vehicle. You can give each one a nickname to keep them organized. When searching for parts, select the active vehicle from the Garage selector in the top navigation to instantly filter results by compatibility.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I view my past orders?">
  Log in to your account, click the **profile icon** in the top navigation, and select **My Orders**. All orders placed while logged in appear here in reverse chronological order. You can filter by date range, order status, or seller to find specific purchases. For each order, you can view tracking, download an invoice, message the seller, or initiate a return.
</Accordion>
