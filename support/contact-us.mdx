AUAPW's customer support team is here to help you resolve issues quickly so you can get the right part and get back on the road. Whether you have a question about an order, a shipping delay, a damaged part, or an account problem, there's a support channel ready to help — and for most order-specific issues, reaching the seller directly is the fastest path to a resolution.

## Support Channels

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Live Chat" icon="comments" href="https://auapw.org/chat">
    Chat in real time with a support agent. Available **Monday – Friday, 8 am – 8 pm ET**. Click to open the chat window and you'll be connected to the next available agent. Typical wait time is under 5 minutes during business hours.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Email Support" icon="envelope" href="mailto:support@auapw.org">
    Send a detailed message to **support@auapw.org** and expect a reply within **24 hours** on business days. Email is ideal for complex issues that need documentation, screenshots, or invoice attachments.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Order-Specific Help" icon="box" href="/account/order-history">
    For questions about a specific order — part condition, fitment, delays — message the seller directly from the order detail page in **My Orders**. Sellers are required to respond within 2 business days.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Returns & Disputes" icon="rotate-left" href="/shipping/returns-policy">
    Initiate a return or open a formal dispute from the **My Orders** page. Select the order, click **Return / Dispute**, and follow the prompts. AUAPW mediates all unresolved disputes between buyers and sellers.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  For most order issues — wrong part, shipping question, condition concerns — messaging the seller directly from your order page gets you the fastest resolution. Sellers have the most context about their inventory and shipments, and many issues are resolved within hours without needing AUAPW support to step in.
</Tip>

## Before You Contact Us

Having the right details on hand when you reach out helps our team (or the seller) resolve your issue on the first response rather than going back and forth. Before you send a message or start a chat, gather the following:

- **Order number** — found in your confirmation email or on the My Orders page (format: `AUAPW-XXXXX`)
- **Part name and part number** — listed on your order receipt and the original product listing
- **Photos of any damage** — if your part arrived damaged, clear photos of the packaging and the part itself are required to process a claim
- **Your VIN** — if the issue is a fitment problem (the part doesn't fit your vehicle), your 17-character Vehicle Identification Number helps the team verify compatibility and identify the correct replacement

<Info>
  You can find your VIN on your vehicle's dashboard (visible through the windshield on the driver's side), on your insurance card, or on your vehicle registration document.
</Info>

## Response Times

Different support channels have different response windows. Choose the channel that matches how quickly you need help.

| Channel | Expected Response Time |
|---|---|
| Live Chat | Immediate (during business hours) |
| Email (support@auapw.org) | Within 24 hours on business days |
| Seller Message (via My Orders) | Within 2 business days |
| Return Request | Seller responds within 2 business days |
| Formal Dispute | Resolution within up to 5 business days |

<Note>
  AUAPW support operates **Monday through Friday, 8 am – 8 pm ET**. Live chat is unavailable on weekends and federal holidays. Email messages sent outside of business hours are answered on the next business day. During peak periods (especially around major holidays), response times for email and disputes may extend slightly — we appreciate your patience.
</Note>
