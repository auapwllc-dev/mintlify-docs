Creating a free AUAPW account puts everything you need in one place — your order history, saved vehicles, return requests, and saved payment methods. You can technically check out as a guest for a one-off purchase, but an account unlocks features that make every future search faster and every order easier to manage. Registration takes under three minutes and requires nothing more than an email address.

## Why Create an Account

An AUAPW account gives you access to a set of tools that make the entire buying experience smoother:

- **Order history** — view every past purchase, reorder a part, or pull up an invoice in seconds.
- **Saved vehicle profiles** — store your year, make, model, and trim so compatibility filtering happens automatically every time you search.
- **Faster checkout** — your shipping address and payment method are saved, so you go from search to order confirmed in just a few clicks.
- **Return management** — initiate and track returns, upload photos, and communicate with sellers directly from your dashboard without hunting for order confirmation emails.
- **Seller messaging** — keep a record of every pre-purchase question and seller response, all tied to the relevant listing.

## Creating Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to auapw.org/register">
    Open your browser and navigate to [auapw.org/register](https://auapw.org/register). You can also reach the registration page by clicking **Sign Up** in the top-right corner of any page on the site. No app download is required — AUAPW is fully browser-based and works on desktop and mobile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill In Your Name, Email, and Password">
    Enter your first name, last name, email address, and a password. Your email address becomes your login username, so use one you check regularly — order confirmations, shipping updates, and return notifications all go there. Choose a strong password at this step; see the [Account Security](#account-security) section below for guidance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Your Email Address">
    AUAPW will send a verification email to the address you provided within a minute or two. Open the email and click **Verify My Email**. This step confirms you own the address and activates your account. If you don't see the email, check your spam or promotions folder before requesting a resend.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Your Profile">
    After verification, you'll be prompted to add a default shipping address and, optionally, your first vehicle profile. Completing your profile now means you won't be interrupted during checkout. You can always add or update this information later from **Account Settings**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Guest checkout is available if you want to place a one-time order without registering. However, submitting a return, opening a dispute, or accessing order history after the session ends all require a registered account. You can convert a guest order to an account order by registering with the same email address used at checkout.
</Note>

## Adding Your Vehicles

Your vehicle profile is the key to getting relevant results fast. Instead of entering your year, make, and model on every search, you save it once and AUAPW applies it automatically as a compatibility filter.

To add a vehicle, go to **My Garage** in your account dashboard and click **Add Vehicle**. You'll select:

1. **Year** — the model year of your vehicle.
2. **Make** — the manufacturer (e.g., Ford, Toyota, GM).
3. **Model** — the specific model (e.g., F-150, Camry, Silverado).
4. **Trim / Sub-model** — important for fitment accuracy, especially for electrical components, body panels, and suspension parts.
5. **Engine** — required for drivetrain and engine-bay parts.

Once saved, your vehicle appears in a dropdown on the search page. Select it before searching and only compatible parts will appear in your results.

<Tip>
  You can save multiple vehicle profiles — there's no limit. If you maintain vehicles for your household, own a fleet, or run a shop, add them all to your garage. Switching between profiles during a search takes one click, so you can quickly find parts for different vehicles in the same session.
</Tip>

## Account Security

Your AUAPW account holds your payment methods, shipping addresses, and order history, so it's worth taking a few moments to secure it properly.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Password Best Practices">
    Choose a password that is at least 12 characters long and combines uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and at least one special character. Avoid using the same password you use on other sites — if another service is compromised, a unique password keeps your AUAPW account safe. Use a password manager to generate and store a strong password so you don't have to remember it.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Email Verification">
    Your verified email address is the primary recovery method for your account. If you ever lose access to your account, AUAPW will send a password reset link to that address — so keep it current. If your email address changes, update it immediately in **Account Settings → Contact Information** and re-verify the new address to avoid being locked out.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Suspicious Activity">
    If you notice orders you didn't place or changes to your account you didn't make, go to **Account Settings → Security** and click **Sign Out All Devices**, then change your password immediately. Contact AUAPW support if you believe your account has been accessed without authorization — the support team can place a temporary hold on the account while you regain control.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
