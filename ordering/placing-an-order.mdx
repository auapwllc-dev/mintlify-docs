Ordering used auto parts on All Used Auto Parts Warehouse (AUAPW.ORG) is straightforward — search for the part you need, confirm it fits your vehicle, and check out in minutes. Every listing comes from a verified salvage yard, so you can shop with confidence knowing the part's condition and seller are clearly displayed before you commit.

## Placing Your Order

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find Your Part and Add It to Your Cart">
    Search by part name, vehicle year/make/model, or part number using the search bar at the top of any page. Once you find the right listing, review the part condition (OEM, used, refurbished), the seller's rating, and any included photos. When you're ready, click **Add to Cart**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Your Cart">
    Open your cart and confirm the details for each item — quantity, condition grade, and the selling yard. If you need more than one of the same part, adjust the quantity here. This is also your last chance to remove an item before entering your shipping information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Shipping Address">
    On the checkout page, enter the destination address where your part should be delivered. Make sure the address is complete and accurate, including any apartment or suite numbers. Residential and commercial addresses are both accepted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a Shipping Method">
    Choose the delivery speed that works best for you:
    - **Standard Shipping** — most affordable, typically 5–7 business days
    - **Expedited Shipping** — faster delivery, typically 2–3 business days
    - **Freight Carrier** — required for large or heavy items such as engines, transmissions, and body panels; a freight quote is calculated automatically

    Estimated delivery windows and costs are displayed for each option before you confirm.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a Payment Method and Confirm Your Order">
    Select your preferred payment method — credit/debit card, PayPal, or Affirm financing. Review your full order summary, including part price, shipping cost, and any applicable tax, then click **Place Order** to complete your purchase.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive Your Order Confirmation Email">
    After your order is placed, AUAPW sends a confirmation email to the address on your account (or the email you entered during guest checkout). This email contains your order number, a summary of your items, and a link to track your order status. Save this email — you'll need it if you have any questions or need to initiate a return.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Ordering Multiple Parts

When you need several parts at once, you can add items from different sellers to a single cart and check out in one transaction. Keep the following in mind:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Separate Shipments" icon="boxes-stacked" href="/shipping/shipping-overview">
    Parts from different salvage yards ship independently. Each seller packages and dispatches their own inventory, so you'll receive multiple shipments with separate tracking numbers if your cart includes items from more than one yard.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Cart Management" icon="cart-shopping" href="/ordering/placing-an-order">
    Use the cart page to review which seller each part is coming from before checkout. Grouping parts from the same seller when possible can simplify tracking and reduce the number of individual deliveries to manage.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Individual Shipping Costs" icon="truck" href="/shipping/shipping-overview">
    Shipping fees are calculated per seller, not per cart total. When ordering from multiple yards, each seller's shipping cost is listed separately in your order summary so you can see the full breakdown before you pay.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Consolidated Order Number" icon="receipt" href="/account/order-history">
    Even when parts ship from different yards, your AUAPW order confirmation ties everything together under one order number. You can view all shipments and their individual tracking details from a single order page in your account.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  Before ordering multiples of the same part from a single seller, check the listing's available stock count or send the seller a quick message through the platform. Some yards list parts based on current inventory and quantities can change quickly.
</Tip>

## Guest Checkout

You don't need an AUAPW account to place an order. Guest checkout lets you complete a purchase using just your email address and shipping information — no registration required.

**How guest checkout works:**
- Enter your email address at the start of checkout
- Complete your shipping and payment information as normal
- Receive your order confirmation email with your order number and a direct tracking link

**Limitations of guest checkout:**

<Info>
  Guest orders are not saved to an account dashboard, which means you won't have access to a full order history, saved shipping addresses, or one-click reordering. If you need to initiate a return, you'll rely entirely on your confirmation email to locate your order details.
</Info>

If you place orders frequently or anticipate needing to return a part, creating a free AUAPW account before checkout is the better choice — it gives you a central place to manage orders, track shipments, and review your purchase history.

---

<Note>
  Large or heavy parts — including engines, transmissions, differentials, and full doors — ship via freight carrier rather than standard parcel services. Freight deliveries are typically scheduled appointments, and you may need to be present to receive the shipment. You'll be contacted by the freight carrier to arrange a delivery window after your order ships.
</Note>

<Warning>
  Orders cannot be modified once they've been placed. If you need to make a change — such as updating the shipping address or removing an item — you must cancel the order within **1 hour** of placement and reorder. After that window closes, the seller begins processing and cancellation may no longer be possible. To cancel, go to **My Orders**, select your order, and click **Cancel Order** before the window expires.
</Warning>
