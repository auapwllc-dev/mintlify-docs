## Payment Security

Your financial data is protected at every step of the checkout process. AUAPW uses industry-standard security practices so you can shop with confidence.

- **SSL Encryption** — All pages on AUAPW use SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) encryption. Data transmitted between your browser and AUAPW's servers is encrypted and cannot be intercepted in plain text.
- **No Stored Card Numbers** — AUAPW does not store your full credit or debit card number on its servers. Payment data is handled and tokenized by a PCI-compliant payment processor.
- **PCI-DSS Compliance** — AUAPW's payment processing meets the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI-DSS), the global benchmark for secure handling of cardholder data.
- **Fraud Monitoring** — Transactions are monitored for unusual activity. If a suspicious charge is flagged, you may be asked to verify your identity before your order is processed.

## Sales Tax

AUAPW calculates applicable sales tax automatically based on the shipping destination you enter at checkout. You'll see the exact tax amount as a line item in your order summary before you confirm payment — there are no surprise charges after you place your order. Tax rates vary by state and locality and are determined at the time of checkout.

## Pricing and Fees

Understanding exactly what you're paying for helps you avoid surprises. Your order total is built from the following components:

| Item | Description |
|---|---|
| **Part Price** | The listed sale price set by the verified seller for the specific part and condition grade |
| **Shipping Cost** | Calculated based on part weight, dimensions, shipping method selected, and delivery address |
| **Handling Fee** | Some sellers charge a handling fee for special packaging (e.g., fragile or oversized parts); displayed in the listing if applicable |
| **Sales Tax** | Applied based on the shipping destination; calculated at checkout |

The full order total — including all fees and tax — is shown on the review screen before you click **Place Order**.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordion title="Why was my payment declined?">
  A declined payment can happen for several reasons unrelated to available funds. Common causes include:

  - The billing address entered at checkout doesn't match the address on file with your bank
  - Your card issuer flagged the transaction as unusual and requires you to verify it (check your bank's app or call the number on the back of your card)
  - The card number, expiration date, or CVV was entered incorrectly
  - Your card has a single-transaction spending limit that the order total exceeds
  - Prepaid debit cards may not be accepted by all payment processors

  If your payment is declined, double-check your card details and billing address, then try again. If the problem persists, contact your card issuer or try an alternate payment method such as PayPal.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I split payment across two cards?">
  AUAPW does not currently support splitting a single order across two separate credit or debit cards. If you'd like to use more than one payment source, consider using PayPal — which allows you to link multiple funding sources within your PayPal wallet — or Affirm financing to spread payments over time with a single transaction at checkout.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="When am I charged — at order or at shipment?">
  For credit and debit card payments, your card is authorized and charged at the time you place your order, not when the part ships. For PayPal transactions, payment is captured immediately upon order confirmation. If you use Affirm, the financing agreement activates at the time of purchase and your first installment due date is set by Affirm's terms.

  If an order is cancelled within the allowable window, a full refund is issued to your original payment method. Refund processing times vary by payment provider, typically 3–7 business days.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do you offer payment plans?">
  Yes — AUAPW offers financing through **Affirm**, a buy now, pay later service that lets you split your order into fixed monthly payments. To use Affirm, select it as your payment method at checkout and complete Affirm's quick eligibility check. Approved customers can choose from available repayment terms. Not all orders or customers will qualify, and interest may apply depending on the plan selected.
</Accordion>
