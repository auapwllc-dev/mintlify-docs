Once you place an order on All Used Auto Parts Warehouse, you can follow every step of its journey — from the moment the seller acknowledges your purchase to the minute the part arrives at your door. AUAPW's order dashboard gives you a live status view, direct links to carrier tracking pages, and email or SMS notifications so you're never left wondering where your part is.

## Tracking Your Shipment

Once your order status changes to **Shipped**, a carrier tracking number becomes available on your order detail page. Follow these steps to see exactly where your part is in transit.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log In to Your AUAPW Account">
    Go to [AUAPW.ORG](https://www.auapw.org) and click **Sign In** in the top-right corner. Enter your email address and password to access your account dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to My Orders">
    From the account dashboard or the navigation menu, click **My Orders**. You'll see a list of all your orders sorted by date, with the current status displayed next to each one.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click on the Order You Want to Track">
    Select the order containing the part you're tracking. The order detail page shows a complete breakdown of items, shipment status, seller information, and shipping address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View the Tracking Number">
    In the **Shipment Details** section of the order page, you'll find the carrier name and tracking number for your package. If your order includes parts from multiple sellers, each shipment has its own tracking number listed separately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Through to the Carrier's Tracking Page">
    Click the tracking number link to open the carrier's official tracking page in a new tab. There you can view the full scan history, estimated delivery date, and any delivery exceptions reported by the carrier.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Sign up for **SMS and email shipping notifications** in your AUAPW account settings. When your order ships, you'll receive an automatic alert with your tracking number and a direct link to the carrier's tracking page — no need to log in to check for updates.
</Tip>

<Note>
  After a shipping label is created, tracking updates can take up to **24 hours** to appear on the carrier's tracking page. If you see a tracking number on your order but the carrier's site shows no movement yet, give it until the following business day before reaching out to the seller.
</Note>

## Tracking Without an Account

If you checked out as a guest, you won't have access to an AUAPW account dashboard — but you can still track your order using your confirmation email.

- Open the order confirmation email sent to the address you provided at checkout
- Locate the **Track Your Order** link in the email body
- Click the link to open your order status page, which updates in real time just like the logged-in dashboard
- Once your order ships, your confirmation email will also receive a separate shipping notification containing your tracking number and a direct carrier link

Keep your confirmation email saved. It is your primary reference point for order status, tracking, and any returns you may need to initiate.

## Freight Shipments

Large parts — such as engines, transmissions, axle assemblies, and full body panels — ship via freight carrier rather than standard parcel services like UPS or FedEx. Freight tracking works a little differently from regular parcel tracking.

**What to expect with freight delivery:**

- After your order ships, you'll receive a **PRO number** (freight tracking number) on your order page and in your shipping notification email
- Use the PRO number on the freight carrier's website to monitor your shipment's progress between terminals
- Before final delivery, the freight carrier will contact you — by phone or email — to **schedule a delivery appointment**. Make sure the phone number on your order is current
- Freight deliveries are made to the curb or loading dock; the driver is not responsible for moving the part inside your home or garage
- If you miss your delivery appointment, the freight carrier may charge a redelivery fee — contact them promptly to reschedule

<Info>
  Freight delivery windows are typically given in half-day increments (morning or afternoon). If you need to change your appointment, call the freight carrier directly using the contact information in your shipping notification.
</Info>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordion title="My tracking hasn't updated in 3 days — what should I do?">
  Occasional tracking gaps are normal, especially when a shipment moves between carrier facilities or crosses regional hubs over a weekend. However, if your tracking shows no movement for more than **3 full business days** after the last scan, take these steps:

  1. Verify the tracking number on the carrier's website directly (not just the AUAPW order page)
  2. Check whether the carrier has posted any service alerts or weather delays in your region
  3. Contact the carrier's customer service line with your tracking number — they can initiate a trace on the package
  4. If the carrier cannot locate the shipment, open a support ticket with AUAPW through the **Help** link in your account or confirmation email. Include your order number and tracking number so the support team can follow up with the seller on your behalf
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="My order shows 'Delivered' but I haven't received it">
  A "Delivered" scan doesn't always mean the package is exactly where you expect it. Before contacting support, try the following:

  - **Check alternate delivery spots** — porches, side doors, garages, mailrooms, or with a neighbor
  - **Wait 24 hours** — carriers occasionally scan packages as delivered slightly before physical drop-off
  - **Verify the delivery address** on your order to confirm the package was sent to the right location
  - **Check with other household members** who may have brought the package inside

  If you've checked all of the above and still cannot locate your package, contact the carrier with your tracking number to file a missing package claim. Then, open a dispute through AUAPW by navigating to **My Orders**, selecting the order, and clicking **Report a Problem**. AUAPW will work with the seller and carrier to investigate and, if warranted, arrange a replacement or refund.
</Accordion>
