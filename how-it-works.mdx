AUAPW is designed to make buying used auto parts as straightforward as shopping for anything else online — but with the safeguards that the auto parts world specifically requires. From the moment you enter a part number or describe what you need, the platform guides you through compatibility checking, seller selection, secure checkout, and delivery, with buyer protections active at every stage. The entire process — from first search to part in hand — typically takes just a few days.

## The Buying Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Search for Your Part">
    Start at [auapw.org](https://auapw.org) and enter the part name, part number, or OEM reference into the search bar. You can also browse by category — engine components, transmission, body and trim, electrical, and more. Results are pulled in real time from the live inventories of verified salvage yards across the country.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Compatibility">
    Before you review prices, confirm the part fits your vehicle. Enter your vehicle's year, make, model, and trim level into the compatibility filter to narrow results to only parts that are confirmed to work with your specific car or truck. Saving a vehicle profile to your account makes this step instant on every future search.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the Listing">
    Each listing shows the part's condition grade, photos, mileage (where applicable), seller rating, and fulfillment time. Read the seller's notes carefully — they'll flag any cosmetic issues, missing sub-components, or installation considerations. You can message the seller directly from the listing page if you have questions before committing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Checkout Securely">
    Add the part to your cart and proceed to checkout. AUAPW accepts all major credit and debit cards, PayPal, and financing options for larger purchases. You'll see a full order summary — including shipping cost, estimated delivery date, and return eligibility — before you confirm payment. Your payment is held securely until you confirm receipt of the part.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive and Inspect">
    Once your part arrives, inspect it promptly against the listing description and photos. Confirm it matches the grade, condition, and any seller notes before installation. If something doesn't match, do not install the part — contact AUAPW support or initiate a return directly from your order dashboard within the return window stated on the listing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Our Seller Network

Every salvage yard that lists on AUAPW goes through a formal verification process before a single part goes live. Sellers are reviewed for business licensing, inventory management practices, and fulfillment reliability. Once active, sellers receive a performance grade — **A through D** — calculated from three factors:

- **Listing accuracy** — how closely parts match their described condition when buyers receive them.
- **Fulfillment speed** — how consistently sellers ship within their stated handling time.
- **Buyer ratings** — aggregated star ratings and written reviews left by verified purchasers.

Sellers whose grades fall below platform standards are placed on probation and removed if performance doesn't improve. You'll always see a seller's current grade on every listing and on their seller profile page.

<Info>
  AUAPW currently has over 500 verified salvage yards in the network, covering all 50 states. Inventory is updated in real time — when a part sells, it's removed from search results immediately.
</Info>

## Buyer Protections

Buying a used part carries inherent uncertainty. AUAPW's buyer protection program is built to minimize that risk at every step.

<Accordion title="Return Policy">
  Most listings include a return window of 30 days from the confirmed delivery date. Parts must be uninstalled and in the same condition as received. To start a return, go to **My Orders** in your account dashboard, select the order, and click **Request Return**. The seller will issue a prepaid return label within two business days.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Warranty Options">
  Many sellers offer optional warranties — typically 30, 60, or 90 days — covering defects and premature failure. Warranty details are listed on each product page before you add to cart. If a warranted part fails within the coverage window, the seller is required to send a replacement or issue a full refund.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Dispute Resolution">
  If a return request isn't resolved within five business days, you can escalate to AUAPW's buyer protection team. Submit your case through the **Open Dispute** option in your order dashboard. The AUAPW team will review listing photos, shipping records, and seller communications and issue a binding resolution — including a full refund if the seller is found at fault — within seven business days.
</Accordion>

<Warning>
  Installing a part before completing your inspection voids your return eligibility. Always verify the part against the listing description and confirm fitment before installation.
</Warning>
