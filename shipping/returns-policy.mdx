AUAPW is committed to making sure you get the right part at the right price. If something goes wrong — whether the part doesn't fit, the seller sent the wrong item, or the part arrived damaged — our buyer protection policy gives you a straightforward path to a return and a full refund. Every seller on AUAPW agrees to honor this policy as a condition of listing on our platform.

## Return Eligibility

Not every return situation is the same. Here's a clear breakdown of what's covered and what isn't.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Eligible for Return" icon="circle-check">
    - The part doesn't fit your vehicle (when you used AUAPW's compatibility checker at the time of purchase)
    - The seller shipped the wrong part
    - The part arrived damaged in transit
    - The part is materially not as described in the listing (wrong grade, missing components, undisclosed damage)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Not Eligible for Return" icon="circle-xmark">
    - Electrical parts that have been removed from their original packaging
    - Parts where you ordered the wrong item without using the compatibility checker
    - Parts that show signs of installation damage (stripped threads, broken clips, evidence of forcing)
    - Parts purchased as "as-is" or "final sale" where clearly marked on the listing
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Warning>
  **Electrical components are non-returnable once removed from packaging.** This includes ECUs, BCMs, sensors, switches, relays, and wiring harnesses. Because electrical parts can be damaged by static discharge or improper handling during inspection, sellers cannot resell them once opened. Verify compatibility before opening the package.
</Warning>

## Return Window

The clock on your return window starts on the date your tracking shows the part as delivered.

- **Standard return window:** 30 days from delivery for most parts.
- **Extended return window:** 90 days from delivery for parts sold with an AUAPW Extended Warranty badge. These parts are covered for fit, function, and undisclosed defects for the full 90-day period.

If your return window has expired but you believe the part was misrepresented, contact AUAPW Support — we review these cases individually.

## How to Start a Return

Starting a return takes only a few minutes from your account dashboard. Have your order number and, if the part arrived damaged, a few clear photos ready before you begin.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log In to Your Account">
    Go to [auapw.org](https://auapw.org) and sign in to your account using your email address and password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to My Orders">
    Click your name in the top-right corner and select **My Orders** from the dropdown menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the Order">
    Find the order containing the part you want to return and click **View Order**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Return Request'">
    On the order detail page, click the **Return Request** button next to the part you're returning.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Your Return Reason">
    Choose the reason that best describes your situation from the dropdown list. Your reason determines who pays return shipping, so select carefully.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload Photos (If Applicable)">
    If the part arrived damaged or is not as described, upload at least two clear photos — one showing the part and one showing any damage or discrepancy. Photos speed up the review process significantly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit Your Request">
    Review your details and click **Submit Return Request**. The seller has 2 business days to respond. You'll receive an email confirmation immediately, and another email once the seller approves the return and issues a shipping label.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Return Shipping

Who covers the cost of return shipping depends on why you're returning the part.

| Return Reason | Who Pays Return Shipping |
|---|---|
| Seller shipped the wrong part | Seller provides a prepaid label |
| Part arrived damaged in transit | Seller provides a prepaid label |
| Part not as described in the listing | Seller provides a prepaid label |
| Part doesn't fit (compatibility checker used) | Seller provides a prepaid label |
| Change of mind / buyer ordered wrong part | Buyer pays return shipping |
| Part doesn't fit (no compatibility check used) | Buyer pays return shipping |

For freight returns (engines, transmissions, etc.), AUAPW coordinates freight pickup directly. Do not attempt to ship a palletized item through a standard carrier — contact AUAPW Support as part of the return process and we'll arrange pickup logistics.

## Refund Timeline

Once the seller receives and inspects your returned part, your refund is issued within **5 business days**. The refund goes back to your original payment method — credit card, debit card, or AUAPW account credit, depending on how you paid.

<Info>
  Your bank or card issuer may take an additional 3–5 business days to post the refund to your account after AUAPW issues it. This processing time is outside AUAPW's control. If your refund hasn't appeared after 10 business days total, contact your card issuer first, then reach out to AUAPW Support if the issue persists.
</Info>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordion title="What if the seller doesn't respond to my return request?">
  If the seller hasn't responded to your return request within **2 business days**, AUAPW steps in automatically. We'll review your request, and if it meets our return eligibility criteria, we'll approve the return on the seller's behalf and issue a prepaid return shipping label. Your refund will be processed as soon as the part is received back at the seller's yard.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I return a part that I installed?">
  In most cases, no. Parts that show signs of installation — including tool marks, lubricant residue, removed protective coatings, or modifications — are not eligible for return. An exception applies if the part failed or was found to be defective during installation and you have not completed the installation. If the part was clearly defective before or during installation, open a return request and include photos documenting the defect. AUAPW will review these on a case-by-case basis.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="The part arrived damaged — what do I do?">
  First, take clear photos of both the damaged packaging and the damaged part before doing anything else. Then log in to your account, go to **My Orders**, and open a return request with the reason "Part arrived damaged." Upload your photos during the request. The seller will issue a prepaid return label and, once the return is received, process your full refund. If the seller doesn't respond within 2 business days, AUAPW will intervene and expedite the resolution.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="I ordered the wrong part — am I still eligible for a return?">
  It depends on whether you used AUAPW's compatibility checker at the time of purchase. If you confirmed your Year/Make/Model/Trim using the compatibility tool and the part still doesn't fit, you're covered and the seller pays return shipping. If you skipped the compatibility check or entered your vehicle information incorrectly, the return may still be accepted within the 30-day window, but you'll be responsible for return shipping costs. To avoid this situation in the future, always use the compatibility checker before adding a part to your cart.
</Accordion>
