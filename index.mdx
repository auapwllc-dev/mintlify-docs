## Get Started in Minutes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    [Sign up for free](https://auapw.org/register) — no subscription required. An account lets you track orders, save searches, and request returns.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search for your part">
    Enter your vehicle's year, make, and model — or paste your VIN — to see compatible parts. Filter results by condition, price, and seller rating.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the listing">
    Check part details, photos, seller grade, mileage, and warranty information before you buy.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Order and track">
    Add to cart, choose shipping, and check out securely. Track your shipment in real time from your order dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>
