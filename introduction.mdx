All Used Auto Parts Warehouse (AUAPW) is a nationwide online marketplace that connects you directly with verified salvage yards so you can find quality used auto parts at a fraction of the cost of new. Whether you're a DIY mechanic tackling a weekend repair, a professional shop sourcing inventory, or simply a car owner looking to save money without sacrificing reliability, AUAPW gives you access to millions of parts from sellers who have been screened, graded, and rated by real buyers.

## What AUAPW Offers

Finding the right used part used to mean calling salvage yards one by one, hoping someone had what you needed. AUAPW changes that entirely. You search once, compare listings from dozens of verified sellers across the country, and order with confidence — all from a single platform.

Here's what sets AUAPW apart:

- **Millions of parts** — engines, transmissions, body panels, electrical components, and more, listed by verified salvage yards nationwide.
- **Part compatibility checking** — enter your vehicle's year, make, and model to instantly filter results to parts confirmed to fit your vehicle.
- **Verified seller network** — every salvage yard on AUAPW must pass a vetting process before listing parts. Seller grades and buyer reviews keep the network accountable.
- **Fast shipping** — most parts ship within one business day, with real-time tracking so you know exactly when to expect delivery.
- **Buyer protections** — clear return policies, warranty options, and a dispute resolution process back every purchase.

## How Buying Works

Getting a part from AUAPW to your garage takes just a few steps. You search for the part you need, use the compatibility tool to confirm it fits your vehicle, review the seller's listing and rating, and check out securely. The seller ships the part directly to your address or chosen shop, and you inspect it upon arrival. If anything isn't right, AUAPW's buyer protection process has you covered.

For a full walkthrough of each step, see the [How It Works](/how-it-works) guide.

<Note>
  Every seller on AUAPW goes through a verification process before their inventory goes live. Salvage yards are graded on inventory accuracy, fulfillment speed, and buyer satisfaction — so you're always buying from a seller who has earned their place on the platform.
</Note>
